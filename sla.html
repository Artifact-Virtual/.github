Service Level Agreement

Last Updated: February 7, 2026 | Version 1.0

This Service Level Agreement ("SLA") describes the service level commitments for Artifact Virtual infrastructure and platform services. This SLA applies to customers with active paid subscriptions.

UPTIME COMMITMENT

We guarantee 99.9% monthly uptime for production services. Enterprise customers receive 99.99% uptime guarantees with enhanced SLA terms.

1. Service Availability

Service Tier Monthly Uptime Max Downtime/Month Credit %
Standard 99.9% 43.8 minutes 10%
Professional 99.95% 21.9 minutes 15%
Enterprise 99.99% 4.4 minutes 25%

2. Service Credits

If we fail to meet the uptime commitment, you may be eligible for service credits:

Monthly Uptime Service Credit (% of Monthly Fee)
99.0% - 99.9% 10%
95.0% - 99.0% 25%
90.0% - 95.0% 50%
Below 90.0% 100%

To receive credits, submit a request to support@artifactvirtual.com within 30 days of the incident. Credits are applied to future invoices and do not exceed one month's service fees.

3. Support Response Times

Priority Description Standard Professional Enterprise
P0 - Critical Production down, no workaround 4 hours 1 hour 15 minutes
P1 - High Major feature impaired 8 hours 4 hours 1 hour
P2 - Medium Feature degraded, workaround exists 24 hours 8 hours 4 hours
P3 - Low General questions, minor issues 72 hours 24 hours 8 hours

4. Support Channels

  • Email: support@artifactvirtual.com (All tiers)
  • Ticket Portal: Available 24/7 for all customers
  • Phone: Enterprise tier only, 24/7
  • Dedicated Slack: Enterprise tier only

5. Scheduled Maintenance

We perform scheduled maintenance during low-traffic windows:

  • Standard Maintenance: Saturdays 02:00-06:00 UTC
  • Emergency Maintenance: As needed with minimum 1-hour notice
  • Advance Notice: 7 days for standard maintenance

Scheduled maintenance is excluded from uptime calculations when proper notice is given.

6. Exclusions

This SLA does not apply to:

  • Features labeled as "Alpha", "Beta", or "Preview"
  • Free tier or trial accounts
  • Outages caused by customer actions or third-party services
  • Force majeure events (natural disasters, war, government action)
  • Scheduled maintenance windows
  • DNS propagation delays
  • Issues caused by customer code or configurations

8. Monitoring & Reporting

We provide:

  • Status Page: Real-time service status at status.artifactvirtual.com
  • Incident Reports: Post-incident reports within 5 business days
  • Monthly Reports: Enterprise customers receive monthly uptime reports
  • API Health Checks: Programmatic access to service status

9. Escalation Process

  1. Level 1: Support Team (initial response)
  2. Level 2: Senior Engineer (within 2 hours if unresolved)
  3. Level 3: Engineering Lead (within 4 hours if unresolved)
  4. Level 4: CTO/Executive (P0 incidents exceeding 1 hour)

10. Customer Responsibilities

To ensure SLA coverage, customers must:

  • Implement recommended high-availability configurations
  • Maintain current contact information
  • Respond to service notifications promptly
  • Report issues through official support channels
  • Cooperate with troubleshooting requests

11. SLA Modifications

We may update this SLA with 30 days notice. Changes will not reduce service commitments during an active subscription term. Continued use after notice period constitutes acceptance.
