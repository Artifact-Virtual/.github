05 — Philosophy

First Principles

01
"If it computes, it will work."
No hand-waving. No hypotheticals. Every system is built to run, measured against reality, and held to the standard of execution — not intention.
02
"Heal faster than you degrade."
Everything breaks. The immune system recovers faster than entropy accumulates. Failure is movement. Stillness is death. Recovery speed is the only metric that matters.
03
"0 = 0. Perfect equilibrium."
The enterprise runs in balance. Two sequential observations yield direction. Intelligence is not complexity — it is the minimum viable pattern that predicts what comes next.
